# Y2 v26 - CRM & Clienteling

## Overview

Customer relationship management and clienteling guide for Cegid Retail Y2 v26. Covers customer records, loyalty programs, price lists, and business conditions.

## Key Takeaways

### Customer Record Tabs
- **General** — Name, contact, address, identifiers
- **Additions** — Additional addresses, contacts
- **Conditions** — Pricing terms, discounts, credit limits
- **Payments** — Payment methods, installment plans
- **Information** — Statistics, history
- **User fields** — Custom fields

### CRM Features
- **Customer identification** — Unique codes, phonetic search
- **Loyalty programs** — Points, rewards, tiers
- **Price lists** — Customer-specific pricing
- **Business conditions** — Payment terms, discount rules
- **Mailing management** — Email, SMS campaigns

### Key Processes
- Batch modification of customers
- Customer deactivation/reactivation
- Customer merging
- Data anonymization (GDPR compliance)
- Business outstanding tracking
