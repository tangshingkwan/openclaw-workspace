# Y2 v26 - Point-of-Sale Management

## Overview

Comprehensive guide for POS Front Office management in Cegid Retail Y2 v26. Covers register settings, receipt configuration, customer handling, and daily operations.

## Key Takeaways

### Register Settings (14 tabs)
- **General** — Code, store, warehouse, default customer
- **Preferences** — Customer entry mandatory, threshold, price update behavior
- **Additions** — Additional charges/fees
- **Management** — Payment handling
- **Discount** — Discount rules
- **Daily operations** — Cash float, opening/closing
- **End of day** — Z-reports, cash reconciliation
- **Layout** — Touch screen, buttons, receipt layout
- **Peripherals** — Scanners, printers, card terminals
- **Communications** — Network, services integration

### Key Configurations
- **Default customer** — Walk-in customer auto-selection
- **Customer threshold** — Mandatory customer entry above amount limit
- **Price update on customer change** — Configurable pricing behavior
- **Register unavailable flag** — Disable register without deleting

### Daily Workflow
1. Open sales day (cash float)
2. Process sales transactions
3. Handle returns, exchanges
4. Close sales day (Z-report)
5. Cash reconciliation
