# Y2 Vertex Tax Connector

## Overview

Vertex tax calculation connector for Cegid Retail Y2. Alternative to Avalara for automatic tax rate calculations.

**Latest Update:** October 23, 2025 | **Version:** 0.0.245 | **Delivered with package:** 25.0.4

## Key Takeaways

### Features
- **Automatic tax calculation** — Real-time tax rates via Vertex
- **Extra Amounts field** — Supports discount amounts (version 0.0.217+)
- **Country & jurisdiction fields** — In response (enhanced in 25.0.2)
- **Supported flows:** Sales, returns, exemptions

### Use Cases
- POS transactions with automatic tax
- E-commerce tax calculation
- Multi-jurisdiction tax compliance
