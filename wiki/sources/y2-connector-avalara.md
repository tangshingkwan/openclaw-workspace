# Y2 Avalara Tax Connector

## Overview

Avalara tax calculation connector for Cegid Retail Y2. Provides automatic tax rate calculations and jurisdiction support.

**Latest Update:** October 23, 2025 | **Version:** 0.159 | **Delivered with package:** 25.0.4

## Key Takeaways

### Features
- **Automatic tax calculation** — Real-time tax rates via Avalara
- **Multi-settings support** — Configurable per store/region (version 0.112+)
- **Default customer support** — From settings configuration
- **Country & jurisdiction fields** — In response (enhanced in 25.0.2)
- **Supported flows:** Sales, returns, exemptions

### Use Cases
- POS transactions with automatic tax
- E-commerce tax calculation
- Multi-jurisdiction tax compliance
