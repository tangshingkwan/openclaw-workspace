## Overview

Plugin for inventory tracking with serial numbers and lot/batch numbers in Cegid Retail Y2 v26.

## Key Takeaways

### Core Methods
- `TrackItem` — Track item by serial/lot
- `GetHistory` — Get movement history
- `GetStockBySerial` — Get stock by serial number

### Use Cases
- Serial number tracking
- Lot/batch number traceability
- Warranty/recall management
