# Y2 v26 - Product Data & Inventory Management

## Overview

Guide for product data and inventory management in Cegid Retail Y2 v26. Covers item records, merchandise types, pricing, and stock handling.

## Key Takeaways

### Item Types
- **Merchandise (MAR)** — Standard products, can be dimensioned (size/color) or single
- **Service (PRE)** — Non-stocked services, repairs
- **Financial (FI)** — Register operations (deposits, cash float)
- **Bill of Materials (NOM)** — Bundled items for sale

### Item Record Tabs
- **General** — Code, description, category, supplier
- **Pricing** — Price lists, margins, currency
- **Characteristics** — Size, color, dimensions
- Other tabs for inventory, logistics, etc.

### Key Settings
- Automatic barcode assignment
- Distinct barcodes per supplier
- Item code assignment rules
- Field customization per record

### Stock Management
- Inventory tracking by warehouse
- Serial number management
- Batch/lot tracking
- Stock valuation methods
