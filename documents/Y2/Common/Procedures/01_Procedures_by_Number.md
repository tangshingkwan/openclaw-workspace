## P302 - Purging Temporary Data from Reports

P302 - Purging Temporary Data from Reports

This management mode includes storing the report in a Cegid Retail Y2 table called YPURESULT. ## P320 - Generating TRV and TRE in Y2

P320 - Generating TRV and TRE in Y2

When a transfer is created between two stores or between two warehouses of the same store, Cegid Retail Y2 systematically creates two documents:

The first document is a sent transfer (type TEM) which corresponds to the exit of goods from the sender warehouse. According to the settings in Cegid Retail Y2, the second document is either a received transfer (type TRE) which corresponds to the receipt of the goods in the recipient warehouse,or a transfer notice (TRV) which corresponds to the goods in transit, and will be validated later by the recipient at the time of receipt. The purpose of this document is to explain the different settings leading to the generation of a transfer notice (TRV) or a received transfer (TRE) after the creation of a sent transfer.

- Product(s): Back Office
- Versions: Y2 version 2018 and later
- Date of update: December 2020
